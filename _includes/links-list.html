Other Student Orgs

  • CS Women
    The women's group at CICS organizes monthly lunches with speakers from academia and industry. They have also run a Technical Workshop Series, organized mentoring opportunities, and work with the new student committee to run the CS Women recruiting dinner.
  • Social
    The social committee organizes social events for CICS graduate students, including libation station rotation, game night, and the new student party.
  • GEO
    CICS students on qualified assistantships are eligible to be members of our chapter of the UAW: the Graduate Employee Organization. The department of computer science may have up to 6 stewards.
  • GSS
    CICS may send two gradaute students as senators to the Graduate Student Senate.
